How to Add Xonra to iOS Home Screen
1

Open Safari

Launch Safari on your iPhone and navigate to xonra.vercel.app

2

Tap the Share Button

Tap the Share button (arrow coming out of a box) at the bottom of the screen

3

Select "Add to Home Screen"

Scroll down and tap on "Add to Home Screen" option

4

Customize & Add

Give it a name (or keep "Xonra") and tap "Add" in the top right

5

Done!

Xonra is now on your Home Screen. Tap it anytime to access the website.
