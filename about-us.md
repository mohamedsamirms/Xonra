
# 🚀 About Xonra

## 👥 Who We Are
We are a dedicated team of developers building games and creative projects. Our mission is to make players happy and excited to play new, high-quality, fun games that people love and can reminisce about from their childhood. 

Over the last two years, we have experimented with multiple games and projects. While most ended up as failed attempts, we continue to strive to create perfect games. We focus on genuine substance rather than just throwing bright colors and popular internet slop around.

---

## 🎯 Our Goal
* **Player Enjoyment:** Ensure players genuinely enjoy trying out our games.
* **Lasting Memories:** Create childhood memories that last forever.
* **Community Building:** Build a community of players who see our work as exciting, promising, and fun. 

> Whether it is a simple game of tag or a complex story mode experience, our ultimate hope is to make you happy while playing.
